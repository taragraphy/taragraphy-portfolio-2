My approach

My goal is not only to create interfaces but to improve the look, UX, and entire design workflow by working closely with the business and its teams, providing high results based on a systematic approach, empathy and high attention to visual design.

Achievements

At Great Simple Studio, I was developing UI kits and design systems driven by the idea of helping designers achieve better results faster. These products are used by companies such as Ideo, Mastercard, Unicef, Salesforce, etc, they were featured on Product Hunt multiple times and gained over 10,000 upvotes in total. Our works were mentioned by such companies as Invision, Marvel, Adobe, etc.

Some of the projects I worked on were featured in curated galleries, such as Behance Interaction, Readymag Explore, Landbook, thegallery.io, Awwwards Magazine
